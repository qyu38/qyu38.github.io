Attending the SPARC Showcase Event 2023

On 10/03/2023, I had the pleasure of attending the SPARC Showcase Event. The event was a fantastic opportunity to connect with leading researchers, industry professionals, and fellow innovators, all focused on driving forward the impact of the SPARC Hub in addressing key challenges in Smart Pavement and Road Construction.

The showcase featured an exciting lineup of presentations and demonstrations, highlighting the latest advancements in pavement technology and smart infrastructure solutions. I had the opportunity to exchange ideas with leading experts and hear about our projects that are shaping the future of road construction and pavement performance. The event not only deepened my understanding of the current trends in smart pavement technologies but also inspired new ideas for my own research in pavement roughness assessment.
