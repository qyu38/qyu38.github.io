Visiting Study at the University of Cambridge

I am excited to share my recent experience during my visiting study at the University of Cambridge. This prestigious opportunity allowed me to engage with leading researchers, delve into advanced topics in pavement roughness estimation, and collaborate with some of the most innovative minds in the field of civil engineering.

Research and Collaboration

During my time at Cambridge, I was able to work closely with experts in the field, expanding my research on smartphone-based pavement roughness assessment. Some of the key takeaways from my visiting study included deepening my understanding of sustainable infrastructure and emerging technologies in road performance evaluation. I had the chance to present my ongoing research and receive valuable feedback from faculty members and fellow researchers, which will undoubtedly enhance my future work.

Looking Ahead

I am incredibly grateful for the opportunity to have studied at the University of Cambridge, and I would like to thank everyone who supported me during this journey, particularly my supervisors and the team at Cambridge. I look forward to applying the insights gained during my stay to my current research and continuing to foster collaboration between our institutions.

Stay tuned for updates on my research as I integrate new ideas and approaches learned during my time at Cambridge!
