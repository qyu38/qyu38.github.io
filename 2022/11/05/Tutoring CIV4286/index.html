Tutoring CIV4286

Date: November 4, 2022  14:10:51 Category: Teaching

In Sep 2022, I supported the online delivery of this unit for students from Central-South University in Changsha, Hunan, China. My responsibilities included composing teaching materials for lectures and practical classes, and assisting students in tutorials with critical path identification and project time management, helping them develop essential project management skills for real-world civil engineering applications. I co-delievered this unit with Dr.Songbo Hu, Tyson Kieu and Yuxi Liu.
