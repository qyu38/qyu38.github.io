Study Melbourne’s G*STEP Program and Partnership with EcoFuture

My experience with Study Melbourne’s G*STEP program has been nothing short of transformative. Through the program, I had the unique opportunity to partner with EcoFuture, an organization focused on resolving large-scale ecological issues driven by climate change and other long-term impacts. This partnership has given me the chance to apply ecological resilience concepts, deepen my understanding of ecosystems, and contribute to solutions for climate-related challenges.

Partnering with EcoFuture through G*STEP has been a game-changer for me. I’ve been able to contribute to projects focused on advancing ecological resilience, system understanding, and the ecological relationships impacted by climate change. These experiences have prepared me to step confidently into the professional world and pursue a career where I can make a real impact.

Find out more and my interview: https://www.facebook.com/watch/?v=596228670786471

What is G*STEP?

Study Melbourne’s G*STEP (Global Skills and Talent Exchange Program) is designed to equip international students with the skills, experience, and networks needed to thrive in the Australian job market. As a participant, I engaged in industry projects, worked alongside professionals at EcoFuture, and built a strong network that aligns with my career aspirations in ecological research and climate action.
